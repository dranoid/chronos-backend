Make a file called dev.env in this folder, with:


 MONGODB_URI = Your mongoDB connection string
 JWT_SECRET = Your jwt secret
USER_EMAIL= the email you are sending from
GOOGLE_MAIL_PWD= the mail password
TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID= the SID of your twilio account
TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN= Auth token from your twilio dashboard
TWILIO_PHONE_NUMBER= phone number purchased from twilio
MY_NUMBER=recipient number or in this case your verified twilio number
